# Original Store Design Analysis (lashonhara.co.il/shop-m/)

## Key Design Elements

### Layout Structure
1. **Top Banner**: Red banner with "משלוח חינם בקניה מעל 100 ש"ח לנקודות האיסוף" message
2. **Featured Products Carousel**: Large carousel at top with product images and "לקניה >>>" buttons
3. **Section Title**: "מוצרים פופולריים" (Popular Products)
4. **Product Grid**: Clean grid layout below carousel

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Red**: #ED1C24 (for banners, buttons, accents)
- **White Background**: Clean white for main content area
- **Black Text**: For product names and descriptions
- **No purple/pink colors anywhere!**

### Product Card Design
Each product card has:
- Product image (square aspect ratio)
- Product name in Hebrew
- Price in ₪ (Shekel symbol)
- Clean white card background
- Simple border or shadow
- Minimal spacing

### Typography
- Hebrew font (likely Assistant or Heebo)
- Bold for product names
- Regular weight for prices
- Clean, readable text

### Featured Carousel
- Large product images
- "לקניה >>>" button in RED
- Navigation arrows (Previous/Next)
- Dots indicator for slides
- Shows 3-4 items at a time

### Product Grid ("מוצרים פופולריים")
- 4 columns on desktop
- 2-3 columns on tablet
- 1-2 columns on mobile
- Equal spacing between cards
- Consistent card heights

### CTA Buttons
- Red background (#ED1C24)
- White text
- "לקניה >>>" text
- Rounded corners
- Hover effect (slightly darker red)

### Free Shipping Banner
- Red background
- White text
- Prominent at top
- "משלוח חינם בקניה מעל 100 ש"ח"
